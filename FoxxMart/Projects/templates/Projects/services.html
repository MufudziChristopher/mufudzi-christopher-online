Products and Services

Services

Governed by our comprehensive Quality Management System, African Utility Suppliers CC offers various on-site, high voltage testing, repair and maintenance services

Established in June 1999 African Utility Suppliers CC started providing on-site testing and repair of telescopic link sticks. Our service offering has grown with various services and products being introduced over the years;

  • On-site testing and repair of telescopic link sticks
  • Calibration of testing & measuring instruments
  • On-site testing and repair of all portable earths
  • On-site inspection repair and servicing of portable ladders
