Welcome

African Utility Suppliers CC is proudly rated as Level 2 BBBEE company, in accordance with the Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment Act 53 of 2003 (BBBEE Act). We are 51% Black Woman owned with a disability.

Established in June 1999 African Utility Suppliers CC started providing on-site testing and repair of telescopic link sticks. Our service offering has grown with various services and products being introduced over the years;

  • 2004 - Calibration of testing and measuring instruments.
  • 2005 - Supply of various consumables including tools, line hardware, ppe, conductors, cleaning equipment and materials, and even supplying clients with coffee, tee, sugar milk, stationary and other needs.
  • 2009 - On-site testing and repair of all portable earths.
  • 2010 - On-site inspection, repair and servicing of portable ladders.

We proud ourselves as a specialist supplier in the industry and with our highly motivated trained staff we always work on new developments and improvements for safer customer demands.

African Utility Suppliers places our customer's safety first!

Vision Statement

To be the preferred supplier of on-site inspection and repair to all high voltage equipment, and to be the preferred provider of quality tools, hardware, personal protective clothing and consumables.

AFRICAN UTILITY SUPPLIERS CC IS COMMITTED TO:

  • Improve customer satisfaction and safety.
  • Improve productivity and service delivery.
  • Improve competency.

MISSION STATEMENT

We know that all the equipment we test and supply are used on a daily basis by employees, and in inherently hazardous tasks, rated safety critical! We therefore undertake;

  • To supply all replacement parts for repairs made only from manufacturers.
  • To communicate any flaws or improvements to our various manufacturers.
  • To meet and exceed our customers service expectations and to timeously and honestly inform them should we not be able to do so.
  • To recognize and respect our customers right of choice (equipment and suppliers) but insist on fair and equitable treatment and recognition for services rendered.
  • To actively engage our customers in exploring ways to build and enhance mutually beneficial “partnerships” and lasting relationships based on trust, reliability and security.
  • To vigorously protect our good name and standing in the market place through innovation, active involvement with and service support to our customers.
  • To continually develop the base of all our employees, foster respect at all levels of the organization and recognize each individuals worth and rights.

QUALITY POLICY STATEMENT

The management and staff of African Utility Suppliers CC are committed to;

  • Provide quality products and outstanding services
  • Continuously develop and improve our quality management system
  • Promote awareness by all personnel that the achievement of quality and customer satisfaction depends on the contribution of each individual.
