Welcome

Established in 1999, African Utility Suppliers CC have provided support to a variety of customers working with high voltage electrical equipment. We provide on-site inspection and repair services to all types of high voltage equipment, as well as the provisioning of quality tools, hardware, protective clothing and consumables.

The company has a Level 2 BBBEE rating in accordance with the Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment Act 53 of 2003 (BBBEE Act), which includes 51 % Black woman owned with a disability.

We proud ourselves as a specialist supplier in the industry and with our highly motivated trained staff we always work on new developments and improvements for safer customer demands.

We are committed to:

  • Improve customer satisfaction and safety.
  • Improve productivity and service delivery, and .
  • Improve competency.

At African Utility Suppliers CC we place our customers safety first.
