What I Do.

I am an IT Creative.
I don't just make websites, I build brands.

Web Application Development

I learnt how to build web applications in Visual Studio. This was also my first opportunity to collaborate in a team on a real world systems development project. Using C#, ASP.NET MVC 4, css, bootsrap, javascript, and the kitchen sink we built a stunning web application that was responsive and user friendly. I am always in development mode because I employ an agile approach when working on projects and tasks. I plan to develop something new and exciting the world's never seen before. Watch this space.


Website Design

I have a sweet tooth for style and presentation which makes me a unique asset when creativity is a requirement. I understand people and what they want out of a website, what attracts them to one and keeps them coming back. I am innovative and not afraid to experiment. All of these talents give me an almost super human ability to consistently deliver a sophisticated, hand-crafted solution guaranteed to make you smile.

E Commerce

I am a programmer that writes programs to make mundane and repetitive tasks less annoying. I learn new languages with ease and grasp concepts and methods quickly. I keep up-to-date with the latest technology trends and news. I have a keen interest in the internet and traverse its furthest reaches on a daily basis.
